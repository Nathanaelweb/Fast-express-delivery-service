Frequently Asked Questions

A tracking number is usually sent to your email once your package has been submitted and verified. It allows you to monitor the status of your shipment. You can also find it in your account on the shipping website or app.

Your tracking information will appear once you input your tracking code correctly. Usually, it takes a few hours after submission for the shipment details to update in the system.

Make sure you input your tracking code exactly as provided, including all letters, numbers, and symbols. If your tracking ID is still not working, please contact your shipper or the online shop where you made your order.

If you do not have a tracking number, we advise you to contact your shipper directly. They can provide you with the tracking code or update you on your shipment status.
